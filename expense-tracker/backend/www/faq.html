Frequently Asked Questions

What is Expense Tracker?

Expense Tracker is a simple tool that helps you log and categorize your daily expenses so you can better understand your spending habits.

Is Expense Tracker free?

We offer a free tier with basic expense tracking features. Premium features may be available in the future.

How do I add an expense?

Once you're logged in, simply enter the amount, select a category, and add an optional note. That's it!

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your expense data at any time. We believe your data belongs to you.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account and all associated data at any time. Just contact us and we'll process your request.

Do you support multiple currencies?

Currently we support single-currency tracking. Multi-currency support is planned for a future update.

How do I contact support?

You can reach us through our contact page. We typically respond within 24 hours.
