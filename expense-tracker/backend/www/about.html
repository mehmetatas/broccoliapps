About Us

Expense Tracker is a simple tool designed to help you understand and manage your personal finances. We believe that tracking your spending shouldn't be complicated.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to make personal finance accessible to everyone. By providing a straightforward way to track expenses, we help you build better financial habits without the complexity of traditional budgeting tools.

Why We Built This

Most expense tracking apps are either too complicated or too limited. We wanted something in between - powerful enough to give real insights, but simple enough to actually use every day.

Built by BroccoliApps

Expense Tracker is built by BroccoliApps, a small team focused on creating useful tools that solve real problems. We're committed to building products that respect your privacy and help you achieve your goals.
